About

Welcome to EcoSteps! This site combines health and technology in one platform — walking isn’t just good for your body, it’s also a way to help our planet by reducing carbon emissions.

My Story

I created EcoSteps because I wanted to improve my fitness while doing something meaningful for the environment. The idea came from Strava, a walking app I use daily for my jogs. Then I thought — if others could see how walking helps the planet, maybe they’d walk more too.
